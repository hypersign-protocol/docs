# SSI and ZKP

> The idea of using zero knowledge proofs to prove claims about aspects of an individual’s identity is a major use case for zk-SNARKs.

Questions like:

* “Are you over 21”?
* “Do you live in the US?”
* “Are you employed?”
* “Are you a real human being?”

Could be answered!
